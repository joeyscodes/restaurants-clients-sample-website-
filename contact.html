Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Walk-ins are always welcome, however we strongly recommend reserving a table — especially on Friday and Saturday evenings when we get fully booked. You can book online, call us or send a WhatsApp message.

Absolutely. We cater for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, nut-free and other dietary needs. Please inform us of any allergies or requirements when booking or when you arrive and our team will take care of everything.

Yes! Our private dining room accommodates up to 30 guests and is perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, corporate dinners and celebrations. Please contact us at reservations@osterian3.cv or call us to discuss your event.

Yes, there is street parking available in the surrounding area of Praia. We recommend arriving a few minutes early during peak hours. Taxis and ride-share services are also widely available throughout Praia.

We kindly ask that cancellations are made at least 2 hours before your reservation time. For large group bookings of 8 or more, we request 24 hours notice. You can cancel by phone or email at any time.

We operate a smart casual dress code. We want our guests to feel comfortable and relaxed while maintaining the elegant atmosphere of Osteria N.3. We kindly ask that beachwear and flip-flops are not worn in the dining room.

We welcome celebration cakes brought by guests — simply let us know in advance. For wine, we have an extensive curated wine list we'd love to share with you. Outside wine is subject to a corkage fee of $15 per bottle.

Currently, Osteria N.3 is a dine-in only experience — we believe our dishes are best enjoyed in the atmosphere we've created. We are exploring delivery options for the future, so watch this space!
