Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Our kitchen is well-versed in vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and nut-free preparations. Please note any restrictions when booking.

Is there a dress code?

We recommend smart casual attire. Many guests choose to dress for the occasion — Monsoon is an experience, after all.

Can I host a private event?

Yes. Our private dining room accommodates up to 24 guests. Contact us directly, and our events team will create a tailored package for you.

Is parking available?

Complimentary valet parking is available directly in front of the restaurant. Self-parking is also accessible in the adjacent lot.
