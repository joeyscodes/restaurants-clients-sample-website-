Our Story

Passion, tradition, and authentic flavors since 2010

La Nostra Storia

Osteria N.3 was born from a simple dream: to bring the warmth and flavors of a traditional Tuscan osteria to our community. Our founder, Chef Antonio Rossi, grew up in the hills of Florence, learning family recipes from his grandmother.

Every dish we serve carries that heritage – from our handmade pasta to our slow-cooked ragù. We source the finest local ingredients and import Italian specialties like San Marzano tomatoes and aged Parmigiano-Reggiano.

Today, Osteria N.3 is a gathering place for families, friends, and anyone who appreciates authentic Italian hospitality. We invite you to sit, savor, and enjoy la dolce vita.
