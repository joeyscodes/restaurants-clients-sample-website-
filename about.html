Who We Are

Born from a Love of Great Food

"We didn't just open a restaurant — we opened a home for flavour, warmth and unforgettable memories in the heart of Praia."

Osteria N.3 was born in 2015 with a single, uncompromising vision: to bring the soul of authentic cuisine to the shores of Cape Verde. What started as a small, intimate eatery has grown into one of Praia's most beloved and recognised dining destinations — but our roots have never changed.

Every dish we serve is a tribute to the tradition, the craft and the passion of our kitchen team. We source our ingredients with obsessive care — locally where possible, always fresh, always chosen for flavour above all else. From hand-rolled pastas to line-caught Atlantic seafood, each plate tells a story.

Our dining room is designed to feel like an extension of someone's home — warm, welcoming and alive with conversation. We believe the finest dining experiences aren't just about the food; they're about the laughter shared, the memories made and the feeling you carry with you long after you leave.

Our Journey

A Decade of Excellence

From a small dining room in Praia to a celebrated Cape Verde institution — here's how we got here.

2015 — The Beginning

Osteria N.3 Opens Its Doors

With 12 tables, a small but dedicated kitchen team and an obsession with authentic flavour, Osteria N.3 opened in Praia, Cape Verde. Within weeks, every seat was taken every evening.

2017 — Growing Recognition

Named Best New Restaurant in Cape Verde

Just two years in, we were honoured with our first major recognition — a nod that validated everything our team had worked tirelessly to build. The queue outside told us what the award confirmed.

2019 — Expansion

Private Dining Room & Extended Kitchen

Responding to growing demand for events and private celebrations, we expanded our space — adding a dedicated private dining room for up to 30 guests and a larger kitchen to match our growing ambitions.

2021 — Innovation

Seasonal Tasting Menu Launched

Our head chef introduced a rotating seasonal tasting menu — a curated six-course journey celebrating local ingredients at their peak. It immediately became one of our most requested dining experiences.

2023 — Community

Cape Verde Culinary Partnership

We formalised our commitment to the local community by partnering with Cape Verde fishermen, farmers and producers — ensuring our menu directly supports those who make our cuisine possible.

2025 — Today

A Decade & Still Growing

Ten years on, Osteria N.3 remains as passionate and driven as ever. With over 5,000 happy guests, a team of 18 dedicated professionals and an ever-evolving menu, the best is still yet to come.
