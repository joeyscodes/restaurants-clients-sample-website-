Who We Are

Born from a Love of Great Food

"We didn't just open a restaurant — we opened a home for flavour, warmth and unforgettable memories in the heart of Praia."

Osteria N.3 was born in 2015 with a single, uncompromising vision: to bring the soul of authentic cuisine to the shores of Cape Verde. What started as a small, intimate eatery has grown into one of Praia's most beloved and recognised dining destinations — but our roots have never changed.

Every dish we serve is a tribute to the tradition, the craft and the passion of our kitchen team. We source our ingredients with obsessive care — locally where possible, always fresh, always chosen for flavour above all else. From hand-rolled pastas to line-caught Atlantic seafood, each plate tells a story.

Our dining room is designed to feel like an extension of someone's home — warm, welcoming and alive with conversation. We believe the finest dining experiences aren't just about the food; they're about the laughter shared, the memories made and the feeling you carry with you long after you leave.
